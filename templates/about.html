About ICD Pregnancy

ICD Pregnancy is a web application that provides a comprehensive database of pregnancy-related codes and explanations. Our goal is to help healthcare professionals and researchers easily access and understand the relevant information they need.

The application allows users to search for specific codes and explanations, as well as download a PDF file containing the full database.

If you have any questions or feedback, please don't hesitate to contact us.
